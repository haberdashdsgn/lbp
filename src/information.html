Trash
Tenants are reminded that it is their responsibility to keep the area outside their space clear of trash and debris. Tenant trash dumpsters must have the lids closed and locked.

Parking
Parking at Lackawanna Business Park is limited to tenants and their customers, and only in designated parking spaces. Cars improperly parked, inoperable, or abandoned will be towed at owners expense.
