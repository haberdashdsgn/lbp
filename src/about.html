LOCATION!

Lackawanna Business Park has 50 suites, and 140,000 square feet of light industrial, warehouse, and general office space.

We are located at 86 Lackawanna Avenue in Woodland Park, New Jersey. The Borough of Woodland Park is one of sixteen municipalities in Passaic County and is about 20 miles west of New York City. Highway access to Lackawanna Business Park is provided by Interstate 80, Routes 3, 23 and 46, the Garden State Parkway, and the New Jersey Turnpike.
